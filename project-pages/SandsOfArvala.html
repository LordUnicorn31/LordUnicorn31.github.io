Sands of Arvala
The mandalorian: Sands of Arvala is a 3D roguelite game made with custom engine.
Project Page
Project Details / Background
I worked as a gameplay designer, designing the player movement and the enemies behaviour mainly. I also worked as level designer. Images below show part of the job done.
As a level designer I made the needed documentation to create the levels, several iterations of blockouts of different levels and the final implementation in the engine with the final props.
As a gameplay designer I worked on the main character, choosing its main mechanics and the player controls, along with creating the personality of the character and the backstory. I also worked on the enemies by changing and adding stats.
The design of the game particles were also done by me.
